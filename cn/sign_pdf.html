❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is my PDF uploaded to your server?

No, absolutely not. All processing happens 100% locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Can I sign multiple pages?

Yes! Each page can have its own signature. Navigate to each page, create your signature, and click "Save Signature to Current Page". The signature will be saved only for that specific page.

Can I reposition the signature?

Yes! After saving a signature to a page, simply click and drag the blue box on the PDF preview to move it. The position is saved for that page.

Does this create a legally binding signature?

This tool adds a visual signature representation. For legally binding digital signatures, you need a certificate-based solution like DocuSign or Adobe Sign.
