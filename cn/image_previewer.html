❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats are supported?

Supports HEIC, LIVP, JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP, WebP, and SVG. HEIC and LIVP (Apple Live Photos) are automatically converted to JPG for preview.

Are my images uploaded to a server?

No, absolutely not. This tool works 100% locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Can I browse subfolders?

Yes! When you select a folder, the tool automatically scans all subfolders recursively and displays images from all nested directories.

How does lazy loading work?

Images are loaded in batches of 30. As you scroll down, more images are automatically loaded. This prevents memory issues when browsing folders with thousands of images.

What happens to Live Photos (LIVP)?

LIVP files contain a still image and a video. This tool extracts the still image and converts it to JPG for preview and export. The video component is not preserved.

How do I export selected images?

Click the checkbox on any image to select it, then click "Export Selected as ZIP". All selected images will be packaged into a ZIP file for download.

Why do some images fail to load?

Some HEIC/LIVP files may be corrupted or use unsupported compression. In such cases, the tool shows a "Preview failed" message. The original files remain unaffected.

Is there a file size limit?

Individual files up to 50MB are supported. Very large files may take longer to process but will still work locally in your browser.
